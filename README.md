### 🛠 My Ideology: Build to Change, Not Just to Ship

My approach is defined by three core principles:

* **Utility > Sophistication:** People don't care how complex your code is; they care if it changes their life. I focus on what a product *does*, not just the tech that powers it.
* **Pivot at Three Days, Not Three Years:** Speed is a tool, not a strategy. If I'm racing on someone else's track—just building a slightly faster version of what already exists—I’ve already lost. I’d rather drop a project in three days and pivot to something that reimagines the possible than spend three years building a "decent" app.
* **The Courage to Try:** The only thing I control is the attempt. I accept that many things won't work, but I refuse to accept the regret of never trying. Every failure is just a data point for the next, bigger build.

**Currently building the future of AI-integrated operating systems.**
